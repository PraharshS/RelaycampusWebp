Questions we get asked a lot...

...but we are happy to answer

What is Relay Campus? What are micro-universities?

Relay Campus is not another online course. Period. Online courses are boring, passive, and lonely and effective only for a few. Relay campus is a platform for top tier talent to join Microuniversities in tech and business.
Micro Universities are university experience recreated online except they are smaller and personalized. It provides personalized mentorship, interactive lectures, and real-world projects.

How does learning happen at Relay Campus?

You will learn in a carefully selected cohort of like-minded individuals. A week at Relay Campus will have a weekly live session, collaborative projects, and stimulating discussions. You will also be provided curated learning materials to help you get a deeper understanding of topics.

Who can join Relay Campus?

Students and young professional (1-3 years of experience) who are interested in making a career in the respective fields. We have a strong screening process to ensure a enriching cohort for Relayers.Ideal for students and professional
a) Considering a move to entry level tech and business roles
b) Tried to learn these but found the existing courses ineffective and boring

What kind of projects will I do?

We believe the actual learning is hands-on learning. You will do one major project and a few mini-projects. Expect the major project to be unique, stimulating, and something that will give your CV serious brownie points.

What mentorship can I expect?

You will receive mentorship from a) one professional from top firms in your field b) Relay Campus Coach. There will fortnightly in-person mentorship from professionals working in top companies and weekly office hours with the Relay Campus Coach.

What career support can I expect?

Our team will go out of our way to help you plan and provide interview support - referrals, warm introductions, interview prep, mock interviews and much more.

Does Relay offer degrees or diplomas? Is there a certificate provided?

We don’t provide degrees or dipomas. However, we do provide course certification and letter of recommendations (restricted to top performers) that would make your CV shiny infront of recruiters.

What will be the profile of my co-learners at Relay Campus?

Your co-learners will be smart and diligent students and professionals, cherry-picked across the country. We have had student leaders from IITs/BITS, startup founders, and who have worked/interned in P&G, Flipkart, ITC, LEK Consulting, VC funds in the past.
